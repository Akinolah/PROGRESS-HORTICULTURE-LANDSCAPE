üîÑThe Process

Our design process begins with a comprehensive consultation where we discuss your vision, preferences, and requirements. This is followed by a detailed site analysis considering factors like soil type, sunlight, drainage, and local climate conditions in Nigeria. We then create customized design concepts, present them to you for feedback, and refine the plans before moving to the installation phase. Throughout the process, we ensure the design aligns with your lifestyle and the unique challenges of the Nigerian environment.

Project timelines vary significantly based on scope and complexity. A free initial consultation can be scheduled within a few days. Design development typically takes 1-3 weeks, depending on the project's intricacy. Installation time ranges from a few days for small projects to several weeks or months for large-scale developments. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout the project to ensure transparency and minimal disruption.

To make the most of our consultation, think about your goals for the space, preferred style or theme, budget range, and any specific features you want (like a vegetable garden, children's play area, or entertainment space). If possible, take photos of the current space from different angles, note any existing plants or features you want to keep, and consider your maintenance preferences. This helps us provide more accurate and personalized recommendations.
