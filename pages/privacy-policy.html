We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal information. Below, you'll find detailed information about our data practices.

Data Collection

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and address to provide quotes, schedule services, and communicate about your landscaping projects.

We also gather usage data through our website, including IP addresses, browser information, pages visited, and time spent on our site to improve user experience and website functionality.

Data Use

Your information is used to provide our landscaping and horticulture services, including creating custom designs, processing payments, scheduling installations, and delivering maintenance services.

We also use your data to send important updates about your projects, respond to inquiries, improve our services through feedback analysis, and send relevant newsletters or promotional materials with your consent.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with trusted partners necessary to deliver our services.

This includes payment processors for secure transactions, suppliers for materials, and subcontractors for specialized work. All partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.

You also have the right to opt-out of marketing communications and withdraw consent for data processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and remember your preferences.

Essential cookies are required for basic website functionality, while analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

NDPR Compliance

We are fully committed to complying with Nigeria's Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

This includes implementing appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, obtaining consent for data processing where required, and maintaining data accuracy.
